How the world will miss its universal education targets

By Josh Holder

The world will be 50 years late meeting its education commitments if current trends continue, with just 11 countries projected to meet the Sustainable Development Goal of of ensuring universal secondary education by 2030.

The projections have been released as part of UNESCO’s 2016 Global Education Monitoring report, which details many of the knock on effects that missed education commitments will have for development.
